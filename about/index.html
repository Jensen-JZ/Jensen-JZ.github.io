Welcome to visit my homepage! I am a senior student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Anhui Agricultural University, and I will graduate from college at the end of June this year. In October 2020, I got the opportunity to study for a master’s degree without an examination. This September, I will enter Hefei University of Technology to continue my studies. This blog I divide it into three types, I will irregularly share some technical articles, insights from my studies, and some interesting things in my life.

Research Interest

  • Behavior analysis and identification of captive animals1
  • Identification of pests and diseases in small samples of cucumber leaves2
  • Orchard fruit identification and localization based on 3D point cloud
